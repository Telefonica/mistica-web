---

**Key design decisions:**

- **`ThemeVariant variant="brand"`** wraps the nav bar and footer, giving them the brand background color from the skin token (`skinVars.colors.backgroundBrand`) without hardcoding hex.
- **`ThemeVariant variant="media"`** wraps the hero so mistica components inside (Tag, buttons) render on a dark/media context.
- **`ThemeVariant variant="alternative"`** alternates content rows (rows 1 & 3 are `default`, row 2 is `alternative`) to showcase light/dark section contrast driven by tokens.
- **`HorizontalScroll noScrollbar`** + a flex container with `gap` handles each card row's scroll behavior.
- **`EmptyState`** with an icon asset renders the "My List" empty state; clicking the "My List" nav link toggles to it.
- All colors use `skinVars.colors.*` — zero hardcoded hex values.
- `ThemeContextProvider` at the root bootstraps the Movistar skin so the file is fully self-contained.
