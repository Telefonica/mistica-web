## Usage

### Use for

- Showing a fixed Movistar brand logo in product headers, navigation, and branded touchpoints
- Keeping brand consistency when the product should not switch logos by active skin
- Applying approved logo compositions and spacing in constrained and responsive layouts
- Using it as an optional home/navigation affordance when logo click behavior is expected

### Don't use for

- Do not alter logo proportions, spacing, or approved variants
- Do not use a brand variant that does not match the product context
- Do not use MovistarLogo in multi-brand contexts that should adapt automatically; use Logo instead
- Do not treat the logo as decorative background content or promotional artwork
